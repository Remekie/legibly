FAQ

Common questions.

What is AEO and how is it different from SEO?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) helps your site appear in Google and Bing. AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) helps your site appear in AI-generated answers — in ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews. Both depend on similar technical foundations, but AEO has additional requirements: your content must be readable without JavaScript, you need a plain-text AI summary file (/llms.txt), and AI must be able to identify your business from structured data. A site can rank well on Google and be completely invisible to AI.

Why doesn't my site show up in ChatGPT or Perplexity?

Usually one of three reasons: (1) Your site requires JavaScript to display — AI crawlers don't run JS, so they see a blank page. (2) Your robots.txt is blocking AI crawlers. (3) Your site has no structured data or AI summary file, so AI engines can't identify what your business does. BlindGEO checks all seven contributing factors in 30 seconds.

Does this work on WordPress? Webflow? Shopify? Squarespace?

Yes — any website. Paste any URL and BlindGEO scans it. For fixing issues, BlindGEO provides a WordPress plugin that applies fixes directly inside WordPress, a GitHub PR for sites with a code repository, and a downloadable fix kit (ZIP with all generated files) for any other platform including Webflow, Shopify, Squarespace, and static sites.

I built my site with Lovable, Bolt, or v0. Is that a problem?

Yes — by default. These tools build React apps that require JavaScript to display content. When an AI crawler visits, it sees an empty page. The Fix plan includes paste-ready prompts specifically for Lovable, Bolt, and v0 — type the prompt into your builder's chat and it applies the fix automatically.

What is an "AI summary" and why does it matter?

/llms.txt is a plain-text file at yoursite.com/llms.txt that tells AI models what your site is about, what you offer, and who you serve — in a format they can read directly. Without it, AI assistants have to read every page on your site to piece together what your business does. With it, they get an accurate summary in one read. BlindGEO generates a custom llms.txt from your actual page content.

How does the WordPress plugin work?

Install the free BlindGEO plugin from wordpress.org. It connects your WordPress site to your BlindGEO account. When BlindGEO finds issues, you can click "Apply fix" and the plugin writes the files directly to your site — /llms.txt, robots.txt AI rules, schema markup in your header. No FTP, no code editing required.

PromptScout and Otterly are monitoring tools — they show you whether your brand is mentioned in AI responses. They don't explain why you're missing, and they don't fix it. BlindGEO diagnoses the technical reasons AI can't read your site and generates the actual fixes: schema markup, robots.txt, llms.txt, and prerender configuration. BlindGEO also shows you which competitors appear instead of you — not just whether you appear.
